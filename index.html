Bitcoin

If you use a self custodial bitcoin wallet, you have full control over your bitcoins. Some of the benefits are:

  • Self custody You own your private keys, which you need to send and receive bitcoins. No one else can access your bitcoins without your permission.
  • UTXO managment Track and spend your coins (UTXO) This allows you to optimize your transaction fees and increase your financial sovereignty.
  • Privacy by design This wallet is designed in client-first design in mind. The application is not dependent on our servers. Choose or setup your own backend. Not your node, not your rules.

Lightning

The Lightning Network is the second layer above Bitcoin's blockchain network, which provides faster, cheaper and more scalable transactions.

  • Embedded node Lightning node is running in your device. You have full control over your node and channels. Not your keys, not your coins.
  • Lightning service provider The LSP connects your embedded node to the lightning network, route payments, and settles your offline payments. LSP provides a bridge between DeFi and your hardly stacked bitcoin.
  • No liquidity managment Avoid inbound liquidity managment and managing channels. Our LSP will do the magic for you and securely routes your payments to your Rootstock account.
